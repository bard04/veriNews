Frequently Asked Questions

What is VeriNews?
VeriNews is a news platform that provides verified, up-to-date news from multiple sources.
How do you verify news sources?
We aggregate news from reputable sources and cross-check for accuracy before publication.
Is VeriNews free to use?
Yes, VeriNews is free to use, and we aim to keep it accessible to everyone.
How often is the news updated?
Our news feed updates every few minutes to bring you the latest headlines.
How can I contact support?
You can reach our support team via the contact page or email us at support@verinews.com.
Do you have a mobile app?
Currently, VeriNews is accessible via the web, but we are working on a mobile app.
Can I advertise on VeriNews?
Yes, we offer advertising opportunities. Visit our advertising page for details.
How can I contribute as a writer?
We welcome contributions! Send your article pitches via our contribution page.
Is my data safe with VeriNews?
We prioritize user privacy and do not share personal data without consent.
How can I report fake news?
If you find suspicious content, use our report feature to flag it for review.
